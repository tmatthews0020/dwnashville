Karran Edge Series

Crafted for 18 gauge, Type 304 stainless steel, all Edge Series sinks are finished with a European brushed satin finish. This elegant finish provides a luxurious look, yet combines the increased durability of a brushed finish.

All double bowl Edge sinks feature a practical lowered bowl divider. For that traditional style of sink, choose one of our 300 Series models. For that more contemporary, commercial styling, a 500 Series model will be the right choice for you.
